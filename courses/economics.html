Economics

The Economics department at Saveetha Engineering College is dedicated to providing a comprehensive understanding of economic theories, principles, and their real-world applications. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with analytical and critical thinking skills, enabling them to analyze economic issues and policies effectively. The course covers various aspects of economics, including microeconomics, macroeconomics, international economics, and econometrics. Students will engage in rigorous coursework, research projects, and practical training to develop a deep understanding of economic concepts and their relevance to contemporary issues. Our faculty comprises experienced professors and industry experts who bring a wealth of knowledge and practical experience to the classroom. The department also encourages students to participate in seminars, workshops, and internships to gain hands-on experience and enhance their employability. Graduates from the Economics department will be well-prepared for careers in academia, research, government agencies, financial institutions, and multinational corporations. They will possess the skills necessary to navigate complex economic environments and contribute to informed decision-making processes in various sectors.
