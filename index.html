Welcome to SEC

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), established in 2001, is a distinguished institution committed to excellence in education and research. With over 35 years of unwavering commitment to providing quality education, SEC has emerged as a leader in engineering education.

SEC offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge required for success in today's competitive world. Our programs are tailored to meet the demands of Industry 5.0, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to tackle the challenges of the future.

At SEC, we emphasize learner-centric education, offering flexible learning options and a supportive environment that fosters innovation and creativity. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty members ensure that students receive a world-class education that prepares them for a successful career.

Vision

To be and to be recognized for setting the standard of excellence in engineering education and high quality research in Science and Technology.

Mission

To promote academic excellence; widen intellectual horizon; self-discipline and high ideals for the total personality development of the individual.
